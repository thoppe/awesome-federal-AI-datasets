# Data-assessment boundary contract

This repository contains catalog metadata, not copies or profiles of the
underlying federal datasets. It may assess manifest integrity and publisher
landing-page observations, but it must not claim content completeness, schema
quality, bias, privacy, or record-level fitness without versioned artifacts,
samples, schemas, or profiling results.

Every reported conclusion identifies its evidence tier: catalog metadata,
publisher documentation, artifact metadata, or sampled/profiled content.
